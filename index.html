6th Annual General Meeting

To be held on 15th October 2023

Greetings to all our esteemed members,
We are delighted to announce that our cooperative society will be holding its 5th annual general meeting at the venue:

28/34-A, Rana Pratap Street No.1, Chajjupur, East Babarpur, Shahdara, Delhi-110032
on Saturday, 15th October 2023
at 10:00 AM.
We cordially invite all of you to attend this important event and participate in the discussions and decisions that will shape the future of our society. As per the notice sent to you on Wednesday, 30th September 2023 via email or speed post to your registered address, the agenda for the meeting is as follows:

  • Confirmation of the minutes of the previous annual general meeting.
  • Presentation and adoption of the annual report and audited accounts for the year ended 31st March 2023.
  • Consideration of any other matter with the permission of the chair.
We look forward to seeing you at the meeting and hearing your valuable inputs and suggestions. Together, we can make our cooperative society stronger and more successful.

Thank you for your continued support and cooperation.
Sincerely,
Navumang Cooperative Urban Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.

Independance Day Celebration

15th August 2015 by Navumang Management Committee.

A small program was organized on the occasion of 69th Independence Day at Saraswati Marg, Chhajjupur on behalf of Navumang Mitra Mandal. In preparation for this program, a video (with patriotic songs) was also shown by the Mandal on the night of 14.08.15, in which the details of the previous efforts of the Mandal were given and the people of the area were invited.

At 7 am on 15.08.15, some neighbours gathered as per schedule. Senior citizen Shri Rajveer Sharma ji hoisted the flag. They all sat together on TV. But watched the Prime Minister's speech live and celebrated by distributing laddoos.

Truly a proud moment for all Indians.
Jai Hind,
Navumang Cooperative Urban Thrift and Credit Society Ltd.

About

Navumang Cooperative (Urban) Thrift and Credit Society came into existence in June 2018 and it has grown leaps and bounds in its service to its community. Strictly based on the very principles of co-operative movement in our Country.

Navumang has completed 5 years of operation and today it is a family of around 100 members.
